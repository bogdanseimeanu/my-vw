Tiguan


A stylish, versatile compact SUV that can fit your friends. And with extra cargo room when you fold the rear seats down in the 5-seater, you can easily pack whatever you need.

Bold, sleek, refined

Capable and sleek. The bold styling and strong, sharp character lines of the Tiguan are sure to turn heads.


An SUV with everything you want, for everything you’ll do

With a spacious 2 cubic meter of cargo room in the 5-seat model when you fold the rear seats down, easily pack the things you need.


Get in your comfort zone

Available V-Tex leatherette seating surfaces and Climatronic dual-zone automatic climate control can make anyone glad to be along for the ride.

So much tech, so easy to use

The Tiguan is equipped with a suite of helpful tech features designed to help make parking, passing, and, well, driving easier.


Customize your view

The available display is a true breakthrough. Instead of the traditional instrument cluster, information can be displayed digitally in high resolution. You can customize what’s displayed, from speed and travel time to route guidance, assistance systems, and elevation.

4MOTION with Active Control

With the turn of a dial, you can switch between four different available all-wheel-drive modes to the one that fits your current driving situation: Snow Mode, On-Road Mode, Off-Road Mode, Off-Road Custom Mode


The power is in your hands

Having eight speeds can mean smooth shifting. In Tiptronic mode, you can shift manually and take control of your driving experience.


Under the hood

We’ve put power behind the performance with a 16-valve, 2.0L, 184-hp turbocharged engine.
