Jetta


The Jetta strikes the perfect balance with a sophisticated look, sleek character lines and a comfortable, modern interior.

Clean lines, fun times

Jetta serves up a sophisticated look that’s as fun to look at as it is fun-to-drive.


Stylish comfort

With a range of available seating options including ventilated and heated front seats, the Jetta is where comfort meets style.


R-Line design

From R-Line badging and black grille accents to the leather-wrapped, multi-function steering wheel, this available trim is the perfect level-up for a car that dares to be bold.

The joy inside the joy ride

The inside cabin’s customizable interior ambient lighting and available panoramic sunroof make for an open and bright interior.


Tech, to help enhance your driving experience

Technology features that help enhance the driving experience. It’s tech with a purpose.


Customize your view

The available display is a true breakthrough. Instead of the traditional instrument cluster, information can be displayed digitally in high resolution. You can customize what’s displayed, from speed and estimated travel time to route guidance, assistance systems, and elevation.

Your drive just got turbocharged

Balancing plenty of torque with smooth handling and efficiency, fun-to-drive has never felt so powerful.


Performance at every turn

From turbocharged performance and handling to driver selection modes, the Jetta has cornered the market on fun.


17" alloy wheels

Compliment the lean, sculpted look of the 2021 Jetta with available 17" alloy wheels.
